Back up to Drive

This app's data lives only on this phone. Download a backup file regularly and drop it into your Google Drive — if you ever switch phones or clear browser data, you can restore everything from that file.

After downloading, open the Google Drive app, hit the "+", choose Upload, and pick the file from your Downloads folder — that's it, your full spending history is in Drive.

Restore from a backup

Pick a backup file you previously saved to Drive (download it from Drive to your phone first, then select it here). This replaces everything currently in the app.
